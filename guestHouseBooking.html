About US

Officers’ Club Dhaka was established in 1967 on 4.5 acres (18,000 m2) land in a picturesque setting at Ramna, Bailey Road, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Its membership is open only to government officers and the officers of semi-government or autonomous bodies.
